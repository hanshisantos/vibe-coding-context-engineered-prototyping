# Exercise 3: Rapid Prototype (Take-Home)

## Overview

Build a reusable prompt template for a real scenario using everything you learned today. This exercise is designed to be completed on your own time.

## Time

20-30 minutes (self-paced)

## Choose Your Scenario

Pick one of these scenarios, or use your own:

### Option A: Study Buddy
Design an AI study assistant that matches your learning style, explains concepts in your preferred way, and respects your knowledge level.

### Option B: Code Reviewer
Design an AI code reviewer that follows your team's conventions, gives actionable feedback, and avoids nitpicking style preferences.

### Option C: Writing Assistant
Design an AI writing assistant that preserves your cultural voice, matches your natural tone, and respects your domain expertise.

### Option D: Your Own Scenario
Pick any real task where context preservation matters.

## Instructions

1. Fill out the prototype template in `prototype-template.md`
2. Test it with an AI tool
3. Iterate at least once based on the output
4. Share it with a colleague and ask: "Does this sound like me?"

## Success Criteria

Your prototype is ready when:

- [ ] Another person could use your template and get consistent results
- [ ] The VIBE elements are explicit, not implied
- [ ] Guardrails are included
- [ ] You have tested it at least once and refined based on output
- [ ] The output sounds like *you*, not like generic AI
