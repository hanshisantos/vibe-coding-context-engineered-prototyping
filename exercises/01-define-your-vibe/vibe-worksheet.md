# VIBE Worksheet

Use this worksheet to define the context for your AI interaction. Fill in each section honestly. There are no wrong answers.

---

## My Task

**What do I need the AI to help me with?**

> [Describe the task in one or two sentences]

---

## V — Value

**What matters most in this interaction?**

> [What is the one thing the AI must get right for the output to be useful?]

**Priority ranking** (number 1-3, where 1 is highest):

- [ ] Accuracy — the facts must be correct
- [ ] Speed — I need a fast first draft
- [ ] Tone — how it sounds matters more than completeness
- [ ] Creativity — I want unexpected or novel approaches
- [ ] Sensitivity — the topic requires cultural or emotional care

---

## I — Intent

**What real-world outcome am I trying to achieve?**

> [If the AI succeeds perfectly, what happens next? Be specific.]

**Who is the audience for this output?**

> [Who will read, hear, or use what the AI produces?]

---

## B — Boundaries

**What must the AI avoid doing?**

> [List at least one thing the AI should NOT do]

**Security check:**

- [ ] This prompt contains no credentials, API keys, or tokens
- [ ] This prompt contains no personally identifiable information (PII)
- [ ] This prompt does not reference confidential or internal-only data

**Content boundaries:**

- [ ] Do not fabricate data or citations
- [ ] Do not use [specific tone or language to avoid]
- [ ] Do not go beyond [specific scope]

---

## E — Expression

**How should the response sound and feel?**

> [Describe the voice, tone, and style you want]

**If I read this output aloud, it should sound like:**

- [ ] A trusted mentor giving advice
- [ ] A sharp colleague in a working session
- [ ] A patient teacher explaining to a beginner
- [ ] A peer reviewer giving honest feedback
- [ ] Other: _______________

---

## My VIBE Summary

Write one sentence that captures your complete VIBE:

> [Example: "I need an accurate, direct summary for my engineering team that avoids revenue data and sounds like a team lead, not an executive."]
