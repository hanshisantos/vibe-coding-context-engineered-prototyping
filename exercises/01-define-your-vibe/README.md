# Exercise 1: Define Your Vibe

## Overview

In this exercise, you will identify the core context elements for a real AI interaction you need this week. This is not hypothetical. Pick something you will actually use.

## Time

6 minutes

## Instructions

1. Think of a task where you need AI assistance this week (writing, coding, analysis, communication)
2. Open `vibe-worksheet.md` in this folder
3. Fill in each section of the VIBE Loop for your task
4. Do not overthink it. Your first instinct captures authentic context.

## Tips

- **Value:** Ask yourself, "If the AI gets everything else right but misses this one thing, is the output useless?"
- **Intent:** Be specific about what changes in the real world when the AI succeeds
- **Boundaries:** Think about what would make the output harmful, embarrassing, or wrong
- **Expression:** Read your last few emails or messages. That is your natural voice. Describe it.

## When You Are Done

You will use this worksheet in Exercise 2 to build a full context-engineered prompt. Keep it open.
